About Us

Our website is all about the tourist spot in Pandan, Antique. It is a beatiful town in the Philippines known for its stunning natural attractions. The town boasts several tourist spot such as the clean and serene Bugang River, the refreshing Malumpati Cold Spring, and the scenic beaches like Duyong Beach and Phaidon Beach Resort. These places are perfect for activities like swimming, river tubing, kayaking, and simply relaxing by the water. The Dioso Farm and Mountain Retreat offer a peaceful escape with hiking and fishing opportunities and more. Pandan is an ideal destination for tourist or nature lovers and adventure seekers looking to unwind and enjoy the beauty of the outdoors.
